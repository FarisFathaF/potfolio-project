"Learn Siemens S7-1200 PLC & HMI from Scratch using TIA"

Through this course, I learned a lot about PLC and HMI using Siemens TIA Portal software. This included learning how to convert schematic diagrams into PLC circuits, programming PLCs using Ladder Logic and SCL coding, various operations in TIA such as bit logic, timers, counters, comparatives, and much more, creating HMIs to integrate with the PLC programs so that user can interact with the PLC programs more easily, simulated various industrial applications using PLC and HMI, and explored many other related topics such as PI (Proportional and Integral) control system.

Certificate of English Proficiency Test (CEPT)

CEPT is an English proficiency test issued by the Center for International Language and Cultural Studies - Universitas Islam Indonesia (Cilacs UII). It assesses a person's skills in both passive and active aspects of the language, covering listening, structure & written expression, and reading. The CEPT test is based on the CEFR (Common European Framework of Reference) guidelines for English proficiency standards. I took the test back in June and managed to score 670 out of a maximum score of 795, which, according to the CEFR guidelines, falls under the B2 level (Upper Intermediate).

Laboratory Assistant - Electric Power System

In 2023 I served as a Laboratory Assistant for the Electrical Power Systems (Sistem Tenaga Listrik/STL) Practicum, focusing on three-phase star-delta motor control, Direct Online (DOL) starter installation circuits, right-left motor control, and load flow and short-circuit analysis using ETAP software. I collaborated with a team to organize the practicum, covering aspects like scheduling, pre-test quizzes, managing attendance and grades, and ensuring all laboratory equipment and tools functioned properly and are in good condition.

Pelatihan Variable Speed Drive (VSD): Classroom Training and Hands-on Practical Setting

I participated in this training from the 10th to the 12th of September. On the first day, we were given a presentation about motors and ABB's VSD products. On the second day, we had the opportunity to perform hands-on VSD operations for the first time. Finally, on the last day, we were assigned a task to practice commissioning ABB's VSD, prepare a commissioning report after, and then present it to the class.
